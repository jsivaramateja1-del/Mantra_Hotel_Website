Our Story

Built on a Vision of Excellence

Royal Haven Hotel was founded in 2005 by the Kapoor Group of Hospitality with a singular mission: to offer every guest an experience that feels both luxurious and personal.

Over the years, we have welcomed dignitaries, business leaders, newlyweds and families from across India and around the world.

Today, Royal Haven Hotel stands as an 85-room luxury destination with an award-winning restaurant, full-service spa, conference facilities, and a team of over 200 professionals.
