What We Offer

World-Class Amenities

Our facilities are designed to cater to leisure guests, business travellers and families alike.

🧖

Luxury Spa

Full-service spa with massages, facials, aromatherapy and body treatments by expert therapists.

🏊

Swimming Pool

Temperature-controlled outdoor pool open 6 AM to 10 PM, with a separate kids' splash zone.

🍽️

Fine Dining

Award-winning restaurant "Zaika" serves Indian, Continental and Asian cuisine for all three meals.

🏋️

Fitness Centre

Fully equipped 24-hour gym with treadmills, free weights, yoga studio and personal trainer on request.

🎪

Conference Hall

Elegant conference and banquet hall accommodating up to 300 guests with AV equipment included.

🍸

Rooftop Bar

Relax with cocktails and mocktails at our rooftop bar while enjoying breathtaking city skyline views.

🚗

Valet Parking

Complimentary valet parking for all hotel guests with secure underground parking facility available.

Airport Transfer

Complimentary airport pickup and drop-off for guests staying 3 or more nights. Book in advance.
